# Lost in The Green

## Prologue

It was finally that day that we were waiting for to finally come. It was now a time to go to the vacation. We often don't believe in any suspicious person telling us to go in some random place but this time we did cause he was providing the tickets and fucking cheap prices.

He didn't even tell us the location of the island .All he gave us where some hilariously nice pictures and videos of the place . The place looked so incredible that it seemed that no one in the world could fight their intrusive thoughts to not listen to this person. My elder brother, Kane, was a man of the army who had just resigned to take care of his children and our family and now works as an automotive technician.

It was only because of him that we had no fear even if anything went bad with us on the island . And now there was the last minute panic. Bags-far too many for just a week- were lined up like brightly colored soldiers by the front door. My partner, Andrew, was doing a final sweep ,ensuring that all the windows and doors were locked. The children already strapped into the car, chanting, "Island! Island!".

The great escape began with a chorus of excited honks and waves to the neighbors.

## The Road Trip

The drive headed deep inland toward a small, regional airport so obscure it barely registered on a map. Suspicion finally surfaced when our convoy of two overloaded vehicles arrived at the airfield; the man who sold us the tickets had never mentioned flying, only that "transport was arranged". The site was eerily quiet, consisting of a single, discreet building and a lone tarmac strip.

There was no waiting around. We were met immediately by a man in a crisp white uniform who directed us past the main gate and straight onto the apron. Waiting there in the dazzling afternoon sun was a sleek, twin-engine propeller plane. It was far more personal than a commercial jet, bearing no markings other than a cryptic, nearly invisible registration number.

"This is... a little extra, isn't it?" Andrew murmured as he helped the children out of the car. Person just shrugged, pointing out that a private jet for the price of a bus ticket wasn't exactly something to complain about.

## The Private Ascend

We boarded the craft immediately. The interior was a display of pure luxury, featuring deep leather seats, polished wood, and a galley stocked with every snack and drink the kids could wish for.

My super curious cousin, Emma, in the thought of exploring the cockpit, bashed the cockpit door just to realize the pilots were already dead and all there was just two terrorists who had hijacked the plane. She was immediately taken as hostage by the terrorists. The echo of her scream traveled through the whole aircraft. After everybody of us heard it, we abruptly rushed to the cockpit.

we startled when we saw the terrorists had their gunpoint at Emma.The terrorists snarled "The moment you think you are smarter than me, she becomes a memory." Kane bellowed "Step away from the girl right now. Otherwise, the only thing waiting for you at the end of this hallway is an introduction to the devil."
